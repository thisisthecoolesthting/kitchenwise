# kitchenwise. Style Kit

## Brand Summary
kitchenwise is a warm, practical app and affiliate review brand for home cooks who care about knives, cookware, appliances, pantry staples, and meal planning.

**Tagline:** Sharper picks for serious home cooks.

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| primary | #C97B5C |
| secondary | #5E6B3B |
| accent | #F6E9C9 |
| background | #FFF8EA |
| text | #1F1A14 |
| muted | #8B7C67 |

Body-text contrast: `#1F1A14` on `#FFF8EA` = **16.34:1**.

## Type Pairing Recommendation
Wordmark/display: Source Serif, Recoleta-like serif, or EB Garamond. UI/body: Public Sans, Inter, or Roboto for recipes and comparison tables.

## Voice & Tone Notes
Warm, practical, slightly nerdy, and never precious. Explain why a pan, knife, or tool changes the cooking experience.

## Do
- Use terracotta, butter cream, olive, and sunlight-on-wood warmth.
- Balance friendly language with real cooking know-how.
- Make buying guidance practical and specific.

## Don't
- Do not sound precious, fussy, or restaurant-snobby.
- Do not overuse rustic farmhouse clichés.
- Do not make pages feel cold or appliance-store generic.
