Mike Southpaw covers kitchen tools for left-handed cooks. That means checking which knives have the correct bevel for a left-hand grip, which peelers work without wrist strain on the wrong side, and which can-openers don't require awkward counter-clockwise cranking. Mike tests every item with his left hand only and flags anything that requires a right-handed workaround.
