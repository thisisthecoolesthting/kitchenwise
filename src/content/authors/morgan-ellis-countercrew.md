Morgan Ellis focuses on compact and counter-friendly kitchen appliances — induction cooktops, juicers, toasters, and other gear where footprint, cord management, and storage clearance matter as much as performance. Every review includes a measured footprint spec and a storage assessment. Morgan does not recommend appliances that require you to rearrange your entire counter to use them.
